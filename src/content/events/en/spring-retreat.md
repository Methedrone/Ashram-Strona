> **Key insight:** The spring retreat is a gentle entry into the rhythm of practice and community life.

The spring retreat in Mąkolno offers a few days of practice in silence and nature. Morning meditation, Karma Yoga, and evening Aarti create a rhythm that helps quiet the mind and open the heart. The program is gentle and especially recommended for first-time visitors.

## What to expect

- Morning meditation and japa
- Shared work as [Karma Yoga](/en/teachings/karma-yoga)
- [Aarti](/en/teachings/aarti-ceremony) ceremony
- Time for rest, reflection, and practice in nature

## How to prepare

Bring comfortable clothes, a sleeping bag, and a mat. We provide simple vegetarian meals and shared accommodation. If you have questions, reach out via [contact](/en/contact).

The spring retreat is a good introduction before the [Summer Retreat](/en/events/summer-retreat) or the [Navaratri Festival](/en/events/navaratri-festival).
