> **Key insight:** The summer retreat is an immersive experience of practice and community life.

We invite you to a two-week summer retreat at Babaji's Ashram in Mąkolno. This is a wonderful opportunity to dive deeper into Babaji's teachings and experience ashram life.

## Retreat Program

**Daily schedule:**
- 5:30 - Morning meditation and japa
- 7:00 - Aarti
- 7:30 - Breakfast
- 9:00-12:00 - Karma Yoga (ashram work)
- 12:30 - Lunch
- 14:00-17:00 - Teachings, discussions, free time
- 18:00 - Evening Aarti
- 19:00 - Dinner
- 20:00 - Bhajans or satsang

**Teaching topics:**
- Truth, Simplicity and Love - foundations of the path
- Karma Yoga as the highest practice ([Karma Yoga](/en/teachings/karma-yoga))
- Mantra and meditation
- Havan and Vedic ceremonies ([Havan](/en/teachings/sacred-fire-ceremony), [Aarti](/en/teachings/aarti-ceremony))

## Accommodation and Food

Simple accommodation in shared rooms. Vegetarian meals (vegan options available). Bring your own sleeping bags and mats.
