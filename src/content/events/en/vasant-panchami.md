> **Key Thought:** True wisdom flows like a calm river when the mind is pure and focused.

Vasant Panchami is one of the most important Hindu spring festivals, celebrated on the sacred panchami (fifth day) of the month of Magha. This day is dedicated to Goddess Saraswati, the patron of knowledge, arts, music, and wisdom. In many regions of India, this day marks the beginning of spring preparations and the new year.

## Program

- 10:00 - Goddess Saraswati Puja
- 12:00 - Bhajans and kirtan
- 13:00 - Communal lunch
- 15:00 - Satsang on the significance of spiritual knowledge

## Spiritual Significance

Vasant Panchami symbolizes the awakening of nature after winter and the beginning of a new life cycle. Goddess Saraswati is depicted holding a white lotus, the vina instrument, and a book - all representing knowledge. Devotees seek her blessings for wisdom in learning, arts, and spiritual practice. The yellow color predominant on this day represents the sun, bringing light and warmth after winter's darkness.

## Information for Participants

All who wish to honor the goddess of wisdom and welcome spring in their hearts are welcome. Please arrive before 10:00 and wear a yellow element as a symbol of joy and spiritual awakening.
